Meet Our Team

Meet the faces behind the success of Prabz - London Tours!

Lauren Barnes - Head Tour Guide

Lauren is one of our fiesty members. Dedicated, confident and a highly motivated person, Lauren has proven to be a great team member. She has been leading many tours around London. As a Londoner herself, she knows her fair share of London's history which makes her perfect for the role of Head Tour Guide!

Steven Lawrence - Second Founder

Steven Lawrence has been friends with Prabz ever since a young age. They've been planing this tour business for a long time and finally with the help of Steven it has become a reality. Steven acts as the second in command of a dream they both once shared. Steven, alongside the CEO Prabz is responsible for managing the company and more importantly making sure that customers have a smile on their faces.
Shelly Cartwright - Head of Directors
Shelly is the big, green...well not green but mean machine! Shelly ensures that we all behave! On a serious note, Shelly is the pride of Prabz - London Tours as she also manages the company and deals with the other directors.
  • Their Mission


Their mission is quite straightforward. They hope to expand the hopes of tourists and fulfill the dreams of tourists everywhere by aiming to provide great tourism and culture through our exquisite tours.
